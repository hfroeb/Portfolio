About Me

Who am I?

I'm Halley, a Back-end Web Developer based in Nashville, TN, with a love for problem solving and a drive to code. I studied Communications, Digital Media Production, and Visual Art at Tulane University. My senior year I took a programming course, inspiring me to move back to Nashville in order to pursue development full time.

During the fall of 2016 I attended The Iron Yard, a three month intensive program, where I studied Back End Engineering with a focus on Java. The curriculum was focused on project-based learning in an Agile environment, with an emphasis on collaboration and strong communication.
